FAQ

Do I need a user account?
No. You do not need a user account for the app at any time.

Is Sleepest free of charge?
Yes, Sleepest is free for everyone. In the future, a free version and a paid version with more features are planned.

Do I have to take my cell phone to bed with me?
No. You can put your cell phone next to the bed or in your room. But then Sleepest can't detect sleep phases.

Does the app have to be active in the foreground?
No. If Sleepest is set up correctly, it does not need to be active in the foreground. Everything necessary is done by Sleepest in the background.

Why is the alarm clock not displayed in the morning?
In most cases, there is a problem with the battery optimization settings on your phone. There are device-specific solutions for this problem. Therefore, follow the instructions that you can find in the app under "Settings -> Help -> Power settings". If you have already done this and it did not solve your problem, you can also contact us via email.

Why is the banner not automatically displayed at my bedtime?
In most cases, there is a problem with the battery optimization settings on your phone. There are device-specific solutions for this problem. Therefore, follow the instructions that you can find in the app under "Settings -> Help -> Power settings". If you have already done this and it did not solve your problem, you can also contact us via email.

Why does the app detect sleep too early?
In a nutshell. You are probably using your phone too little. The app can't determine the time to fall asleep if the user is using their phone too little. Good news, we are planning an alternative "I'm going to sleep" feature in the future.

Why are the detected sleep states so strange?
Actually, we only have device data to define the user's sleep state. If the detected sleep states are definitely wrong, you could move your phone to a different position to make the sleep detection more reliable.
