FAQ

Do I need a user account?
No. You dont need a user account at any time for the app.

Is Sleepest for free?
Yes sleepest is free for everybody. In future there is a free and a paid version with more functions planned.

Do I have to take my mobile phone to bed with me?
No. You can put your phone next to the bed or in your room. But then Sleepest cannot recognize any sleep phases.

Does the app have to be active in the foreground?
No. If set up correctly Sleepest do not have to be active in the foreground. Everything necessary is done by Sleepest in the background.
