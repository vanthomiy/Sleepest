Why you should choose Sleepest

Sleepest redefines the alarm clock. Adapted to mobile working hours and new ways of life, the alarm clock can wake you up within a period of time and not at a specific time.

Background

The app works completely in the background. It does not have to be opened at any time.

Auto detection

Automatic sleep detection. You don't have to tell the app that you are going to sleep.

Battery friendly

Hardly any battery consumption. In contrast to other sleep trackers, no charging necessary!

Secure

The data is stored only on your local device and can always be deleted or exported by you.

The smartest alarm clock you've ever had

Set your desired sleep duration and you will be woken up within your wake-up time window after your desired sleep duration.

Track your sleep behaviour

You can compare the duration and sleep phase distribution for a week or a month. The algorithm recognizes light, deep and dream sleep phases.
