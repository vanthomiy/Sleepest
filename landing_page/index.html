Be Sleepest.

Sleepest redefines the alarm clock. Adapted to mobile working hours and new ways of life, the alarm clock can wake you up within a period of time and not at a specific time.

Why this app ist simply the best out there?

- Hardly any battery consumption: In contrast to other sleep trackers, no charging necessary! - Automatic sleep detection: You don't have to tell that you are sleeping now! - The app works completely in the background: the app does not have to be in the task manager - The alarm clock can wake you up after your desired sleep duration

Smart.

Set your desired sleep duration and you will be woken up within your wake-up time window after your wanted sleep duration.

Automatic.

The app automatically detects your sleep. You don't have to open the app or tell the app that you are sleeping. All of this is automatically detected by algorithms.

Powerful.

You can look at your sleep cycles for each night. You can also compare the duration and sleep phase distribution during the week or month. The algorithm recognizes light, deep and rem sleep phases. Sleepest uses various sensors on your mobile phone to detect the individual sleep phases.

Secure.

Your data is always safe and in your own hands. The data is stored only on your local device and can always be deleted or exported by you.

How The App Works?

Download

Download the app in the playstore of any of your android devices.

Setup

Make an inital setup in the app to provide necessary information for the app to detect your sleep correctly.

Use

Now you dont have to open the app again. Everything will work in the background and the app does not require to be opened.

Sleep better

Use the app to reach your desired sleep duration. Also you can track your sleep behaviour and get information about your sleep phases.

The target group.

- You want to record and view your sleep behavior in an uncomplicated way and only with your mobile phone - Getting enough sleep is important to you, but you don't want to go without an alarm clock - You want an alarm clock that has adapted to the new work and living environment - You want to go to bed whenever you want and be woken up by an alarm clock the next morning when you are well rested.
